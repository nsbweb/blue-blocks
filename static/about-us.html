About US

School Ethos

A Montessori school in spirit and letter, a Cambridge affiliated international school, Blue Blocks offers education that is the best of both worlds.

Blue Blocks was established in 2009 in Hyderabad with the vision to provide a rich and resourceful schooling environment.. Blue Blocks follows the Montessori philosophy of guiding a child to knowledge and is affiliated with Cambridge School Education to impart the curriculum for grades 1 to 10.

The school empowers the child with self-confidence and independence to develop a well rounded personality.

Our focus is on giving children the freedom to choose what they want to learn and promote their decision making skills. We believe experience is the best teacher and strive to make each day memorable for the child.

CAreer with Blue Blocks

Montessori trained educators have the unique opportunity to change the education system for the better. They can reform the thinking and perception of what should be the ideal schooling environment.

Montessori Directress: Looking for AMI Certified individuals to be directresses of the Self-Interpretive Environments (3-6 year Primary environment).

Elementary Directress: Looking for AMI Certified individuals to work with the 6-12 year old children in the elementary environment.

Assistants: Graduates in any field with good command over language and keen interest in working with children.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Motivated to work with Children
  • Open to learning.
  • Understanding of the psychology of the 3-12 year olds.
  • Pleasant personality and soft demeanour
  • Good logical and analytical skills
  • Good communication skills
  • Basic computer skills
  • Age 20 years and above
  • Graduate in any stream

Montessori teachers act as guides to a child’s independence. The teacher gives each child a presentation of a concept using specifically designed equipment.
