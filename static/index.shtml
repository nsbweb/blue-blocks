BLUE BLOCKS

SELF LEARNING PRE SCHOOL

Blue Blocks is a Montessori and Cambridge Affiliated School for ages 1 to 15. Our primary programme follows the authentic Montessori pedagogy and curriculum. Our Elementary school is the perfect amalgamation of the Montessori and Cambridge curricula.

Our High School which will be operational from 2022 will offer Cambridge Curriculum with Montessori Erd Kinder adolescent programme.
